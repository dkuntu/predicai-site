Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Academy, the Accelerator, and Shield.

Academy vs Accelerator
What is the difference between the Academy and the Accelerator?
The Academy is an application-only Builder Program for individuals ($149). It provides a structured curriculum (Notion-based) to help you architect AI systems independently.

The Accelerator is a high-touch Premium service for teams and executives. It includes 1:1 strategy, custom tooling builds, and governance audits. Think of it as hiring a Fractional AI Officer.
Do I need coding skills for the Builder Program?
No. The Academy is designed for "Domain Experts" (lawyers, operators, marketers) who want to architect systems using natural language, not code.
Shield & Safety
Does Shield protect against voice cloning?
Yes. Shield provides specific "Safe Word" protocols and callback scripts designed to break the urgency of AI voice scams.
Why is Shield a subscription?
Because safety isn't static. Scammers change tactics rapidly. Your subscription covers the ongoing research we do to keep your family's protocols updated against the latest threats.
