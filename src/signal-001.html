Issue 001 — Jan 10, 2026

Don't trust AI summaries by default. They sound smooth but miss where people actually disagree. Use tools that help you review, not just rewrite.

01 Mandate

Stop treating AI as a search engine.

Vague prompts create a "Faster Mess." This week's update focuses on catching mistakes early—when they are still easy to fix. If a tool can't show where people disagree, don't use it for important decisions.

04 One move to steal

Add one "review move" before you send something out. Force the AI to find the tension points:

Read this from three different points of view. Where would they disagree? What would each person worry about?
