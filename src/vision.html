Statement of Intent

1. Human Agency First.
We believe AI should expand human capability, not replace human judgment. We build tools that keep you in the loop, not out of it.

2. Verify, Then Trust.
Blind trust in automation is a liability. We teach "Trust but Verify" protocols designed for high-stakes environments.

3. Long-Term Thinking.
We don't chase hype cycles. We build protocols (like the 7 Gates) that will still be valid even when the models change.
