Voice Defense

The Voice Clone Threat

Remove clean voice samples from your public voicemail and establish family callback rules to force verification.

⚠️
Audio Source Risk: Voicemail

Personal greetings provide scammers with a clean sample of your voice. Switch to a system default greeting to reduce exposure.

Step 1 Reset Your Greeting

Switch to the System Greeting (the automated voice that reads your number).

The Safe Script “You have reached [Read Phone Number]. Please leave a message.”
Step 2 The Family Safe Word

Establish a word not found on social media to verify identity during emotional or urgent calls.

Step 3 In-The-Moment Script
If you get a suspicious call “I’m going to hang up and call you back on your saved number. What’s the Safe Word?”
Do

Hang up, call back, ask Safe Word.

Don't

Stay on line or use provided numbers.
