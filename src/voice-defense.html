Voice Defense Protocol

How to stop AI voice cloning scams in 3 steps.

1. Establish a Safe Word

Pick a word that is unique but easy to remember (e.g., "Popcorn" or "Blueberry"). Never share it online.

2. The Challenge Rule

If you get a distress call from a family member, ask for the Safe Word immediately. AI cannot know it.

3. Hang Up & Call Back

If they "can't remember" the word, hang up. Call their known phone number directly.
