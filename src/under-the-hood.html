Under the Hood

What Predicai is built with — and why it matters

Not a claim of partnership or endorsement. A transparent acknowledgment of the systems behind the work.

Predicai is not a demo. It is a working system—designed, tested, and maintained across real tools, real constraints, and real tradeoffs.

What you see across the site, mailers, and knowledge systems is the output of an intentional architecture. Below is a transparent view of how that system is structured.

Core Intelligence Layer

  • Large language models
  • Custom prompts and evaluations
  • Human-in-the-loop review
Why it matters: Reliability over novelty.

Workflow & Automation

  • Automation systems
  • Form logic and routing
  • Explicit human checkpoints
Why it matters: Handoffs done right.

Content & Knowledge Systems

  • Structured knowledge base
  • Playbooks and shared assets
  • Versioning and change tracking
Why it matters: Trust through context.

Delivery & Distribution

  • Email infrastructure
  • Landing pages and gated resources
  • Minimal, intentional analytics
Why it matters: Clarity in delivery.

Security & Privacy

  • Data minimization by default
  • No training on user data
  • Explicit safeguards and review points
Why it matters: Safety by design.
Privacy & Safety Pledge

This is not fine print. It is a design principle.

Predicai is built to reduce risk, not maximize data collection.

  • User submissions are never used to train models
  • Data is not resold or repurposed
  • Only the minimum information required is retained
  • Human judgment is applied where decisions matter
Trust is not a policy.
It is a system property.
