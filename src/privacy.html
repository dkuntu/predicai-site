Privacy & Data Governance

Effective Date: December 31, 2025

At Predicai™, we treat data governance as a capability, not just compliance. This policy explains how we handle information across Predicai services.

Privacy Pledge™

Your submissions are used only to deliver the service you requested.

  • No selling of personal data.
  • No public-model training on your artifacts.
  • No advertising use of your submissions.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name and email for access.
  • Payment Data: Processed by Stripe; we do not store card numbers.
  • Submissions: Text or images provided for analysis.

2. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed. You can request deletion of your artifacts at any time by emailing hello@predicai.ai.
