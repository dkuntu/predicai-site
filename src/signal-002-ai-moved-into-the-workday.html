Issue 002 — Jan 17, 2026

AI quietly moved into the workday. It's no longer a place you go; it's a helper inside the tools you already use.

01 Mandate

Stop treating AI like something you’ll “learn later.”

This week, AI showed up inside normal work: meetings, documents, and notes. The skill gap isn't about being "behind"—it's about the work moving.

04 One move to steal

Turn one real task into a learning loop. Pick a meeting recap or a follow-up email and use this prompt:

Explain this task back to me in plain language. What are you assuming? What would make this go wrong? Now ask me 2 questions before you help.
